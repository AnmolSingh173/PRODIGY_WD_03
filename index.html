TIC TAC TOE GAME

How to play ?

The aim of the game is to make either 3 Xs or 3 Os in a row on 3 by 3 grid . At the start of the game , you have to choose either you will take X or O . You will be playing against an AI in the game , you can also play against other player if you want . Where the other player will manually pick the place where they will place their X/O as per their selected choice.
